Versmes Valda is a specialist investment management firm focused on the structuring, development and acquisition of hard asset businesses across energy, critical materials manufacturing, and related industrial sectors.

Our Focus Areas
What We Do

Three Pillars of Expertise

We combine deep sector knowledge with financial and legal structuring capability to unlock value in complex, asset-intensive businesses.

01
Deal Structuring

We design and execute bespoke capital structures for complex transactions — integrating equity, debt, and hybrid instruments to optimise risk-adjusted returns across the asset lifecycle.

02
Business Development

We take an active role in building and scaling hard asset operations — from project inception and permitting through to operational ramp-up, delivering commercial and technical value creation.

03
Mergers & Acquisitions

We source, evaluate and execute M&A transactions in industrial sectors where physical asset quality and regulatory positioning are decisive — bringing disciplined valuation and hands-on integration capability.

How We Work

A Rigorous, Asset-First Methodology

01
Asset Quality First

Every investment thesis begins with a granular assessment of the underlying physical assets — reserves, condition, regulatory standing, and competitive positioning within the value chain.

02
Integrated Business Intelligence

We apply deep business analytics across every phase of the investment cycle — from market and competitive mapping to operational benchmarking and performance monitoring. Our intelligence layer transforms raw commercial and technical data into actionable insight, sharpening both entry decisions and value creation execution.

03
Legal & Regulatory Navigation

Complex industrial assets carry complex legal frameworks. We manage permitting, licensing, corporate structuring, and cross-border regulatory compliance as an integral part of value creation.

04
Long-Term Value Partnership

We are operators and co-investors, not advisors at arm's length. Our alignment with partners and projects is substantive — we remain engaged through development, operation, and exit.

Where We Invest

Sectors of Focus

We concentrate in sectors characterised by physical scarcity, infrastructure permanence, and strategic importance — where patient capital and specialist knowledge create durable competitive advantage.

01
Onshore Energy Production
Geothermal energy development and heat extraction, geological hydrogen prospecting and production, and hydrocarbon field development and asset acquisition across the Baltic and Central European region.
02
Critical Materials Manufacturing
Foundry, metallurgical, and advanced manufacturing businesses producing materials of strategic and defence significance.
03
Industrial Infrastructure
Hard infrastructure assets with regulated or contracted revenue streams — including logistics, processing, and storage facilities.
04
Defence-Adjacent Industries
Manufacturing and supply chain businesses serving defence and dual-use applications, benefitting from NATO-driven demand growth in the Baltic region.
